What I Learned:

When I created this game it was the largest programming project I had undertaken to that point. Ultimately, it was technically a failure, as I didn't complete my goal of finishing it in one month, but I consider it a success in how much it taught me. I learned the importance of maintaining a consistent art style, keeping my code organized, and how important daily habits are to a project.
