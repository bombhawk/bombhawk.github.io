What I Learned:

This project is actually my second time remaking Wordle. I first did so back in 2022, when the game was at its peak popularity. Remaking a project like this showed me how far I truly have come. What I chipped away at over the course of roughly two weeks originally I made in about two days the second time around. Although I didn't learn too much from this project, it showed me how far I have come in the past few years, something I belive is invaluable.
